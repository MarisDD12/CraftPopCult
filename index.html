About Mari

Hey there! I'm Mari, a crafter who loves creating everything from crochet projects to vinyl designs. With over 15 years in the hospitality tech industry and a passion for hands-on creativity, I bring a unique perspective to crafting.

I believe that crafting should be accessible, fun, and just a little bit magical. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned pro, you'll find tutorials and resources here to spark your creativity.

When I'm not crafting, I'm probably hanging out with my dachshund mix puppy, creating relaxing content, or designing digital products for my Etsy shop!
